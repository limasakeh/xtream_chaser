FACTIONS

WHITE LOTUS SOCIETY

The White Lotus Society was jointly founded by Raiden, the immortal protector of Earthrealm, and the Shaolin Order of Light sect, following after the death of the Great Kung Lao at the hands of Goro. Since the sorcerer Shang Tsung had finally defeated the longtime champion with a recruit from Outworld, it became apparent that Earthrealm was vulnerable to attack, not just by native enemies, but those from foreign worlds as well



Sub-Zero, Scorpion, and Raiden are in this faction.

SPECIAL FORCES

The Special forces was initially formed in the United States of America as the United States Special Forces with the goal of stopping international crime gangs. Now, the organization has become the Earthrealm Special Forces. While it was still an anti-crime organization, it's mandate had expanded to defending all of the Earthrealm against other dimensional invasions, such as by the Tekunin



Jax, Johnny, Kenshi, and Ermac are in this faction.

THE BROTHERHOOD OF SHADOW

The Brohtherhood of Shadow is an unholly, fanatic cult centered on the demon sorcerer Quan Chi and worships the fallen Elder God Shinnok. It's countless members are ever scheming and planning for his release from the Netherrealm. It's trademarks are stealth, secrecy, and deception. The brotherhood is also involved with black magic and necromancy. They achieved their goal when Quan Chi was able to get hold of the sacred amulet needed to free Shinnok from his eternal prison, from where he already controlled the entire Netherrealm.



QuanChi, Shinnok, and Reptile are in this faction.
